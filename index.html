ABOUT ME

I'm Avipsa, a 3rd-year CS student at Kathmandu University, and the person behind Aerie Studio. I started freelancing a few months ago with a love for clean, minimal design to showcase a brand's aesthetic and I've been building in public ever since.

My approach is simple: every project starts with understanding who the brand actually is, then designing something that feels intentional, not templated. I work across web design, graphic design and brand identity, and I'm just as comfortable in Figma as I am writing the code myself.

I'm early in this journey, and I mean that with confidence, not apology. Every project I take on gets real attention and real care.
